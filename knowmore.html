Know More About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It has a diverse atmosphere, ecosystems, and climates that support a wide variety of lifeforms. The Earth has one moon and is 4.54 billion years old.

The planet is covered with 71% water, mostly in oceans. The rest is continents and islands. Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen. The magnetic field protects the planet from harmful solar radiation.
